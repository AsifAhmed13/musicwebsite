What is TuneIn?

TuneIn is an application that let's you upload, store, and play all of your music from the cloud.

How do I add music?

First, create a new album by filling out the form on this page. Once an album is created you will be able to add/upload songs.

What are some Album logo best practices?

  • Uploading a Logo is Optional.

  • Have a resolution of 512x512

  • Use a common image formats such as .JPG, .GIF, or .PNG

  • Remain under the 2MB limit.

  • Square images look best
